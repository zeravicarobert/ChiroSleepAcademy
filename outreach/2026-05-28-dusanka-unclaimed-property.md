## Considerations Robert should think about

1. **Are there other heirs?** If Robert has siblings, they may also
   have an inheritance claim to this property under CA intestate
   succession (if Dusanka died without a will). For property under
   ~$184K, the Small Estate Affidavit procedure (CA Probate Code
   §13100) lets heirs claim without full probate — but typically
   requires all heirs to sign.
2. **Estate size.** If Dusanka's total CA estate is over $184,500
   (2022 threshold), full probate may be required. If under,
   simplified procedures apply.
3. **Tax implications.** Inheritance proceeds aren't taxable income
   to the heir, but any later income from inherited assets is.
4. **Timeline.** CA Unclaimed Property claims typically take 90-180
   days from filing to payment. Plan accordingly.
5. **Worth getting probate guidance.** Especially if amount turns
   out to be substantial. A CA probate attorney consult ($200-$500)
   may save thousands by ensuring the claim is filed correctly the
   first time.
