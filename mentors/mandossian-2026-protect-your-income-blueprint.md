## The Opportunity

### Executive Summary

Dr. Z is uniquely positioned to build a highly profitable, referral-driven
disability certification and worker recovery business focused on California SDI
evaluations and support.

Unlike traditional chiropractic practices that depend on insurance billing or
local advertising, this model is built around:

- High urgency
- Strong financial pain
- Remote scalability
- Referral partnerships
- Predictable lifetime value

> The business is not fundamentally a chiropractic practice. It is an
> income-protection and recovery guidance business.

### The Market Opportunity

California workers contribute to SDI through payroll deductions, yet many:
- do not understand their benefits,
- do not know how to navigate the system,
- or cannot find providers willing to properly complete documentation.

Dr. Z fills that gap by offering:
- disability evaluations,
- SDI certification guidance,
- ongoing support,
- and recovery coaching.

## Why This Model Works

**High Trust + High Urgency = Fast Decision Making**

- confused by the system,

First and primary niche, because they:

- already contribute into SDI,

- and have strong urgency around lost income.

---

## The Acquisition Strategy — Borrowed Trust

The fastest path to patient acquisition is **not** cold advertising. It is
**referral trust transfer.**

### Primary Referral Channels

**1. Disability Attorneys** — clients already need documentation; attorneys
value responsiveness and reliability.
- Build attorney referral relationships
- Host educational lunch-and-learns
- Create a simple referral intake process
- Provide rapid communication and turnaround

**2. Union Representatives & Shop Stewards** — offer educational
presentations: *"How California Workers Lose Income Unnecessarily."*
Objective: help workers preserve income legally and appropriately.

**3. Urgent Care & Occupational Clinics** — Dr. Z becomes the downstream
specialist.
- Referral kits
- Intake forms
- One-page process overviews
- Direct scheduling access

## Final Recommendation

For the next 90 days: **focus narrowly.**

**Primary objective:** become the trusted SDI and worker recovery specialist
for California union and labor-related referrals.

Do not attempt to market broadly. Instead:
- dominate one niche,
- simplify the message,
- build referral trust,
- create operational consistency.

> That is the fastest path to predictable patient acquisition and scalable growth.
